Spider Man

About

Spider Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published byMarvel Comics. Created by the writer-editor Stan Lee and Stan Ditko, he first appeared im the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 (August 1962) in the comic book Silver Age of Comic book.He has been featured in comic books, television shows, films, video games etc.

Creation and Development

In 1962, with the success of the Fantastic Four, Marvel Comics editor and head writer Stan Lee was casting for a new superhero idea. He said idea for Spider-Man arose from a surge in teenage demand for coimc books and desire to create a character with whom teenager could identify.As with the Fantastic Four, Lee saw an opportunity "to get out of his system" what he felt was missing in comics books.
